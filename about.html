Rick Suarez

Rick has been working with us for 5 years now. His secret is passion for what he does. He loves most outdoor activities you can name, but his favorite has always been river rafting and kayaking. He's one of our best guides that can keep you informed and entertained as you go through your adventures.

Joshua Garcia

Safety is always our first priority. Same is the case for Joshua. He's one of our adventure guides and he's the one in charge of our emergency team. He also loves the great outdoors but his strength shows because of his much experience in rescue operations in the past.

Jennifer Gomez

With her bright smile, and witty jokes, adventures become moments to be remembered. Jennifer works both as a guide, and a first-aider, and she often accompanies large groups of adventurers in their journey. She makes sure that everybody in the team is well-trained and ready for the fun ahead.
